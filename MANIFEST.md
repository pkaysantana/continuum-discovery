# Continuum Discovery: Biodefence by Design

**Mission:** Architect a self-sustaining discovery engine for AMR and NTD countermeasures, starting with high-affinity binders for the H5N1 Hemagglutinin head domain (PDB: 2IBX).

## Vision & Alignment

This project is deeply aligned with **Sustainable Development Goal 3 (Good Health & Well-being)**, specifically focusing on combating communicable diseases and strengthening capacity for early warning, risk reduction, and management of national and global health risks.

As highlighted in the GRAM (Global Research on Antimicrobial Resistance) report, AMR is a leading cause of death globally. Our goal is to accelerate the development of countermeasures to stay ahead of natural and engineered pathogen evolution.

## Approach

By integrating state-of-the-art computational biology tools (via the Amina platform) with autonomous agentic workflows, we aim to drastically reduce the time from target identification to validated candidate. Our initial focus on H5N1 serves as a proof-of-concept for a broad-spectrum rapid-response pipeline.
